About Me

I'm Bhavana Mehta, currently a PhD student at the University of Pennsylvania, advised by Dr. Boon Thau Loo and Dr. Mohammad Javad Amiri. My research focuses on developing machine learning-based adaptive systems, blockchain, and fault-tolerant distributed databases.

Previously, I worked as a Design Engineer at Bluespec Inc., optimizing RISC-V cores and automating hardware generation processes.
